How to Embed

Copy the iframe snippet from any widget above and paste it into your HTML. Each widget is a self-contained page that loads its own data, requires no external scripts, and refreshes automatically.

<iframe
  src="https://terminalfeed.io/embed/btc-price?theme=dark"
  width="320"
  height="120"
  frameborder="0"
></iframe>

Customization

  • ?theme=dark or ?theme=light to switch color schemes
  • Adjust width and height to fit your layout
  • Widgets scale to fill their container, so percentage widths work too
  • Crypto Movers works best at 180px height; Service Status at 160px
  • All other widgets fit comfortably at 120px height
