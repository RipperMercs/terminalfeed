What Is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores data as a pattern of black and white squares. QR codes can encode URLs, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, and more. They are scanned using smartphone cameras and are ubiquitous in payments, marketing, authentication, and logistics.

What Can You Encode?

This generator supports plain text, URLs, email addresses (mailto:), phone numbers (tel:), WiFi network credentials, and vCard contact cards. Use the preset buttons to get started with common formats.

Privacy

QR codes are generated 100% client-side in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your text never leaves your machine.
