>_ Features

TerminalFeed is a free, real-time command center dashboard with over 30 live data feeds. Everything streams in real-time using WebSockets, Server-Sent Events, and smart polling. No login required. No paywall. Here is every data feed available on the dashboard.

Financial Data

Bitcoin Price

Real-time BTC/USD price via WebSocket from CoinCap and Binance. Includes an embedded TradingView chart with multiple timeframes, 24-hour change percentage, and market cap. Updates every second with true streaming data.

Stock Market Movers

Top gaining and losing stocks from a curated list of 25+ major tickers including AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, and more. Shows real-time price, percentage change, and volume. Data sourced from Finnhub and Yahoo Finance.

Cryptocurrency Rankings

Top 30 cryptocurrencies by market cap with real-time prices, 24-hour change, and market cap values. Highlights the biggest movers of the day with color-coded gain/loss indicators.

BTC Network Stats

Live Bitcoin network data from mempool.space including current block height, mempool size, network hashrate, difficulty adjustment countdown, and fee estimates for high, medium, and low priority transactions. Learn what each metric means in our guide: How to Monitor Bitcoin Network Health.

Whale Watch

Monitor large Bitcoin transactions flowing through the mempool in real-time. See whale-sized transfers as they happen, with transaction values displayed in BTC and USD.

Crypto Market Overview

Total cryptocurrency market cap, 24-hour trading volume, BTC and ETH dominance percentages, and total number of active cryptocurrencies. Powered by CoinGecko.

Fear & Greed Index

The Crypto Fear & Greed Index measures market sentiment on a scale of 0 (extreme fear) to 100 (extreme greed). Useful for gauging whether the market is overheated or presenting buying opportunities. Read our full guide: What is the Crypto Fear & Greed Index and How to Actually Use It.

Forex Heatmap

15 major currency pairs against USD displayed as a color-coded heatmap. See at a glance which currencies are strengthening or weakening, with percentage changes updated throughout the trading day.

News & Community

Tech / AI News

Top stories from Hacker News filtered for technology, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and developer content. Each story shows title, source, points, comment count, and time posted. Tagged by topic (AI, BTC, Markets, Tech, Dev).

Reddit Tech

Trending posts from top technology subreddits including r/technology, r/programming, r/machinelearning, and more. Shows post title, subreddit source, upvotes, and comment count.

Show HN / Ask HN

Community-driven posts from Hacker News Show HN and Ask HN sections. Discover what developers are building and what questions the tech community is asking.

Bluesky Trending

Trending posts from the Bluesky social network, the decentralized alternative to Twitter/X. See what the tech community is discussing on the open social web.

Stack Overflow Hot Questions

The hottest programming questions on Stack Overflow right now. See what developers worldwide are struggling with and discussing, with view counts, answer counts, and tags.

Developer & Open Source

GitHub Trending

Trending repositories on GitHub by star count. Discover the most popular open source projects, with language, star count, description, and daily star gains.

GitHub Live Events

Watch the world code in real-time. A live stream of GitHub events including pushes, pull requests, stars, forks, and issues from repositories across the platform.

Dev/Ops Status

Real-time operational status of major developer services including GitHub, Cloudflare, OpenAI, Anthropic, npm, Vercel, Discord, and more. Know instantly when services go down.

Product Hunt

Today's trending products on Product Hunt. See what new tools, apps, and services are launching, with upvote counts, descriptions, and categories.

AI Model Leaderboard

Rankings of the top large language models by ELO rating. Compare GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Mistral, and other frontier models across benchmarks.

Science & Earth

Earthquake Monitor

Live earthquake data from the USGS showing all M2.5+ seismic events worldwide. Displays magnitude, location, depth, and time. Color-coded by severity with the most significant events highlighted.

Global Disaster Alerts

Real-time alerts from the Global Disaster Alert and Coordination System (GDACS), operated by the United Nations and European Commission. Covers earthquakes, tropical cyclones, floods, volcanoes, and droughts worldwide.

Weather

Local weather based on your IP geolocation with animated visuals — rain, snow, sun, thunderstorms. Shows current temperature, conditions, humidity, wind speed, and forecast. Animated weather scenes bring the dashboard to life.

Space Launches

Upcoming rocket launches from rocketlaunch.live. See launch provider, mission name, rocket type, launch site, and countdown timer. Never miss a SpaceX, NASA, or other launch again.

Solar Weather

Space weather data from NASA's DONKI system including recent solar flares, coronal mass ejections, and geomagnetic storm alerts. Know when aurora might be visible or when solar activity could affect communications.

Knowledge & Culture

Wikipedia Live Edits

A real-time stream of Wikipedia edits via Server-Sent Events. Watch as editors around the world update articles in real-time. See the article title, edit size, and whether content was added or removed.

Wikipedia Featured Article

Today's featured article from Wikipedia, with a summary and link to the full article. A daily dose of curated knowledge from the world's largest encyclopedia.

Trending Books

Currently trending books from Open Library. Discover what the world is reading right now, with titles, authors, and cover images.

This Day in Tech

Historical tech events that happened on today's date, plus a rotating tech term of the day. Learn the history behind the technology we use every day.

UAP/UFO Sightings

Historical UAP and UFO sighting data from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC). Browse reported sightings with date, location, shape, and description. Because the terminal watches everything — even the skies.

Lifestyle

Sports Scores Ticker

Live and recent scores from major sports leagues including NBA, NFL, MLB, and NHL. Displayed as a scrolling ticker bar across the top of the dashboard, powered by ESPN data.

Podcasts

Latest episodes from top tech and business podcasts. Stay current with the best audio content from the tech world without leaving your dashboard.

Daily Recipes

A random recipe of the day from TheMealDB, complete with ingredients and instructions. Even terminal operators need to eat.

World Clocks

Current time in 8 major cities worldwide with business hours indicators. See at a glance whether markets are open and when your colleagues across time zones are working.

Dashboard Features

Internet Pulse

Real-time latency measurements to global endpoints. A quick health check showing whether the internet is performing normally or experiencing issues in different regions.

Customizable Layout

Drag, reorder, hide, and show any panel. Save custom layouts, export them, share via URL, or choose from presets like Trader, Developer, and Crypto. Press C to open the Panel Manager or E to enter organize mode.

Self-Healing System

When an API feed fails or goes offline, TerminalFeed automatically hides the affected panel and restores it when the data source recovers. The dashboard stays clean and functional even when individual feeds have issues.

PWA Support

Install TerminalFeed as a Progressive Web App on your desktop or mobile device. Works offline with cached data, launches from your dock or home screen, and receives background updates.

All data feeds are free and require no account or API key. TerminalFeed runs entirely in your browser with no backend server required. Data is cached locally for fast loading and resilience. Want to build with this data? Check the free API or read about all 30+ APIs we use.
